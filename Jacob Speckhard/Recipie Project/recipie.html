Recipe For Breakfeast Chili

Ingredients:

  • 2 boxes of Frosted Flakes
  • 3 poptarts of different flavor
  • 1 egg
  • a piece of toast
  • 1/3 cup Orange juice

Procedure:

  1. Crush up peice of toast and stir with 1/3 cup of Orange Juice.
  2. After stiring for 30 seconds put in an egg.
  3. let sit for 1 hour
  4. slowly pour in frosted flakes while stiring gently
  5. crush up poptarts and add in to the mix
  6. put in microwave for 1 min
  7. Let sit until cooled down
